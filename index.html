How to Play Battleship
Welcome to the Battleship game! The board is a 7x7 grid where three ships are hidden. Each ship occupies 3 consecutive cells either horizontally or vertically. To make a guess, enter a combination of a letter (A-G) and a number (0-6) (e.g., A3, D5) and click the "Fire!" button. If you hit a ship, the cell will be marked, and you'll get a message. Your goal is to sink all three ships by hitting all their parts. Once you have guessed all three ships three times, the game will display your total guesses and you will need to refresh the page (press F5) to play again. Good luck!
